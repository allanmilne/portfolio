About Me

A few things about me

BIO

After many years of programming industrial CNC machines to create physical components for customers, I opted to make a career switch and pursue a passion for all things tech. Inspired by the progress I had made in my spare time with the The Odin Project, I knew I needed to take my journey into software development seriously.


I signed up to CodeClan, a 16 week intensive coding bootcamp based in Edinburgh, and thrust myself into a torturous, intensive yet wonderfully fulfilling learning experience. Now, armed with a well rounded technical skillset, a thirst for continuous learning and a passion for code based problem solving, I am ready to begin my career as a developer.
