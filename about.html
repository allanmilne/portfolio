BIO

I’m a Software Developer with a background in Engineering, programming industrial  CNC machines that produce physical components for customers.

In late 2018 I made the move to self-employment as a Matched Bettor. This sparked an interest in how software works and what it can achieve. I began learning to code in my free time and, inspired by my progress, I decided to take my interest to the next level and enrolled in CodeClan's Professional Software Development course.

Now, having graduated with a well rounded technical skillset, the proven ability to build production-ready applications and a thirst for continuous learning, I am ready to begin my career as a software developer.
